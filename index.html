About

jscaylum is a 16-year-old independent artist from England making music from a small, shared bedroom — and meaning every word of it.

Growing up with divorced parents, carrying anxiety and depression, and feeling things far too deeply — that's where the music comes from. Not a studio. Not a team. Just a bedroom, a lot of honesty, and nowhere else to put it.

Still small. Still growing. But the songs are real, and that's always been the point.
