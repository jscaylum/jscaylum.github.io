// about

jscaylum is an independent artist from England writing music about the things most people don't say out loud. Heartbreak, self-doubt, the kind of trust that gets broken and never quite heals the same way. Honest to the point of uncomfortable — and that's exactly the point.

For anyone whose thoughts are hell and feels it all a little too deeply.
