// about

jscaylum is an independent artist from England whose music dives deep into the deep and emotional corners that people (such as himself) may face from time to time. Drawing from personal struggles with isolation, identity, and emotional pain, jscaylum crafts songs that feel like diary entries set to sound — honest, vulnerable, and impossible to ignore.

Thoughts are hell, huh? For anyone who has ever gone through anything ever, even during happy moments, his music expresses what's been held on deep inside.
